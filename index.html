Tammy is a tech defector turned stand up comic searching for the meaning of life in cartoons and sour gummies as a queer troublemaker in America. She's born and raised in California, spent 5 weeks in Fiji digging holes for Jesus, and now residing in Sonoma County.

Like her hero Bullwinkle, she fumbles through life following her heart into awkward situtions. She was married, went to college, and got a job before realizing who she was before getting fired, squandering her degree, and getting divorced.

She did get her daughter in the divorce, part time as well as the student loans. She lives to code but doesn't want to live in the Silicon Valley mental box.

Her recent time in customer service helped her understand her love of working with people, so she set her stable well paying career failures on hold to actualize her dream of making people laugh... for what she's saying.
